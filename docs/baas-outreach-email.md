**Subject:** White-label BaaS partnership — neobank for San Marino (Crest)

Hello [PROVIDER] team,

I'm [YOUR NAME], founder of **Crest** (crestpay.app) — a digital wallet being built for
residents of the **Republic of San Marino**, a market that EEA-passported providers
(Revolut, Wise, N26) don't serve because San Marino sits outside the EEA.

We're looking for a licensed partner to provide IBANs, card issuing and SEPA payments
under a **white-label** model. Four questions are decisive for us:

1. **Company onboarding** — Can you onboard a company **incorporated in San Marino** as a
   partner, or would you require an EU/EEA-incorporated entity?

2. **End-customer coverage** — Can you serve **end customers resident in San Marino**
   (outside the EEA) on an ongoing basis, as account/card holders under your licence?

3. **San Marino regulator (BCSM)** — Serving San Marino residents is a reserved activity
   under San Marino law. Foreign licensed institutions can operate there under the
   "provision of services without permanent establishment" regime, including via a local
   agent. Are you **already registered with the Central Bank of San Marino (BCSM)**, or
   **open to registering**, with Crest as your agent/distributor?

4. **White-label & pricing** — Do you offer **full white-label** (cards and app under the
   Crest brand, you invisible to the end customer), or API access under your brand? And what
   are the indicative costs (setup, monthly minimum, per-card / per-transaction fees)?

A bit about us: pre-launch, waitlist live at crestpay.app, target market ~34,000 residents,
product = personal IBAN + virtual/physical card + instant transfers. Initial volumes: a
pilot of a few hundred users, scaling from there.

Could we book a short intro call to assess feasibility? Thank you — looking forward to your reply.
